About us

Independent Workers & Advisors represent an increasingly large segment of the professional workforce. Advisory services are expected to continue to grow by 3.7% to 5.8% per year over the next years. At the same time, automation is expected to transform the way we work, faster than ever before. Since the 1970s certain types of transactional jobs have declined by more than 50% due to automation. There will be fewer jobs. And more of the remaining jobs will shift to digital.
