How does it work ?

start /
To start, you just need an email adress to create your profile. Then, follow the next steps to highlight your valuable work experience: select among different projects or missions that you had to manage..
play /
You will receive targeted pitches after fulfilling your ID information. If you are interested, you can answer, ask more information to the Contractor, challenge him, reshape the pitch in order to make the deal. Give the best of yourself to be selected !

You can find on the platform several learning programs to improve your competencies.
